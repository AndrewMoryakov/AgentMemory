# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want AgentMemory to be a constructive, technically serious, and respectful open-source project.

Participants should:

- argue from evidence
- be direct without being hostile
- assume good faith where possible
- keep discussions grounded in user value and engineering quality

## Expected Behavior

- be respectful in issues, pull requests, and discussions
- give clear technical feedback
- keep criticism focused on the work, not the person
- avoid harassment, abuse, and personal attacks

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment or intimidation
- hateful or discriminatory language
- repeated bad-faith disruption
- doxxing or publication of private information

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

For serious concerns, contact the maintainer through the security/support path documented in this repository.
